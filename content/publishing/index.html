--- title: publishing description: How to publish your PlayCanvas game topic: publishing index: 1 --- # Publishing Your PlayCanvas Game Once you have built your game, you will no doubt want to publish it and allow people to enjoy its majesty! PlayCanvas makes things very easy for you. You have a choice in how you do this: 1. [Publish to PlayCanvas](/publishing/playcanvas.html) 2. [Self-host on your own web-site](/publishing/selfhosting.html) 3. [Publish to Google Play or the iOS App Store](/publishing/appstores.html) 4. [Publish to Firefox OS](/publishing/firefoxos.html)
