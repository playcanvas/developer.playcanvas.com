--- title: content creation description: tags: - section topic: content creation index: 1 --- # Content Creation PlayCanvas supports the importing of a wide variety of content types: * Images (PNG, JPG, GIF). * Models and animations (built in most mainstream 3D content creation packages). * Audio samples. Once imported into your online project, the content is made avaiable to your project as assets. The content creation guide shows how to: * Install and use the [PlayCanvas Asset Tools][asset_tools]. * [Build content][build] so that it is PlayCanvas-friendly. * [Preview content][preview] rapidly in the browser before importing it. * [Import content][import] into your online project.
