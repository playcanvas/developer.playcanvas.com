--- title: controls description: topic: playcanvas designer subtopic: controls index: 2 --- # PlayCanvas Designer Controls ## Camera Controls The Designer's Camera is controlled with the Mouse and Keyboard
Alt + Left Mouse ButtonRotate Camera; Orbit Focus
Alt + Middle Mouse ButtonPan Camera
MousewheelZoom in/out
## Mouse Controls
Left Mouse ButtonSelect Entity
## Keyboard Controls
FFocus on selected EntityLock camera rotation on the selected Entity
WTranslate ModeChange gizmo to translate
ERotate ModeChange gizmo to rotate
RScale ModeChange gizmo to scale
